Our Services

Borehole Drilling

Zuri Drilling Company is one of the leading borehole service providers in Kenya. We provide a world-class borehole drilling services to industrial, commercial, agricultural etc.

Neema and warma assessment permits

Our company specializes in an environmental impact assessment, which is carried out to determine the impact of drilling a borehole and the mitigation measures put in place.

Tank Towers

We offer: Crane services, fabricate containerised Homes and offices, CNC plasma cutting services,Steel Tanks/Towers, Warehouses/Godowns etc.

Hydrological survey

Before borehole drilling it’s very important to conduct a Hydro-geological Survey in your proposed site. Generally groundwater is unequally distributed ZURI DRILLING CO ascertains suitable sites for exploration of significant quantities of groundwater before carrying out any drilling exercise

Test pumping and water analysis

We can test water from wells, boreholes, springs, rivers and other private water supplies for the most common forms of bacteria and chemicals.

Borehole Flushing

Our range of services includes Borehole Flushing, Borehole Installations, Test Pumping, Fishing, Pump Lowering and Lifting etc
